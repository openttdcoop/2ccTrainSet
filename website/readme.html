3. Loading speed on passenger cars

In this set do we have 3 different "styles" of passenger cars, and they all have different loading speed based on their role in real life

Type Loading Speed
Indiactor		 Passengers
per tick		 Typical Capacity
Per Unit		 Typical
Running speed
InterCity Trains Low 8 per tick 40-50 125mph/200kmh or higher
Commuter Trains Medium 20 per tick 45-120 60-100mph/100-160kmh
Metro Trains None, own system 60 on them all! +-150 Below 60mph/100kmh

Notes:
* All non-MU passenger coaches are concidered IC-coaches.
* Most of the EMU's have powered wagons. In theese cases will the coaches also
add the running cost shown in the information about the particular engine.
* Most MU's can also take mail. The attached wagons will have aprox 75% of their rated PAX-capacity
* Metro's cant take mail.
